Kashmir Itinerary

Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip to Kashmir:

  • Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar, check-in to hotel, visit Dal Lake.
  • Day 2: Explore Mughal Gardens (Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, Chashme Shahi), visit Shankaracharya Temple.
  • Day 3: Excursion to Gulmarg, enjoy Gondola ride, explore local markets.
  • Day 4: Visit Pahalgam, enjoy scenic beauty, visit Betaab Valley, Aru Valley, and Chandanwari.
  • Day 5: Travel to Sonamarg, enjoy breathtaking landscapes, visit Thajiwas Glacier.
  • Day 6: Return to Srinagar, visit Hazratbal Shrine, explore local markets for shopping.
  • Day 7: Departure from Srinagar.

Feel free to customize this itinerary according to your preferences and interests. Don't forget to indulge in the delicious Kashmiri cuisine and interact with the warm and hospitable locals!
